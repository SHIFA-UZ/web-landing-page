About Shifa

Evolving the healthcare ecosystem

Shifa is reshaping care by aligning the needs of patients and clinicians in one trusted, modern space. By removing friction from access, documentation, and communication—and elevating context with responsible AI—we help the system work the way it should: faster decisions, clearer care journeys, and a healthier, more resilient healthcare industry.

For Patients & Doctors

Built to connect doctors and patients seamlessly.

Making care faster, clearer, and more secure. Book visits or video calls without the wait, keep your health records safe and accessible, and give clinicians the context they need to treat you with confidence. With Shifa AI supporting every step, care becomes simpler for patients and smarter for doctors.

Shifa AI is your real-time healthcare co‑pilot. For patients, it streamlines bookings, surfaces the right information, and keeps documents organized so decisions are clearer. For doctors, it brings context forward—summarizing history, capturing notes during consults, and generating reports—so care is faster, safer, and more informed.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

At SHIFA (shifa.uz), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, the legal basis for processing, and your rights.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this website is:

  • SHIFA
  • Hansastraße 116, 13088 Berlin, Germany
  • Email: contact@shifa.uz

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

2.1 Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content
  • Your preferred language (for our response)

This data is sent to us via email and is not stored in a database. We use it solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Server Logs (cPanel / Web Server)

Our hosting provider automatically collects standard server access logs, which may include:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages requested (URL)
  • HTTP status code
  • Browser user-agent string
  • Referring URL

These logs are retained by our hosting provider for security and operational purposes and are automatically deleted after 30 days. We do not use these logs for profiling or marketing.

2.3 Analytics (Umami)

We use Umami Cloud for privacy-friendly website analytics. Umami:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not collect personal data or IP addresses
  • Does not track users across websites
  • Does not use fingerprinting

Umami collects only anonymous, aggregated metrics such as page views, referrer sources, browser type, and country (derived from IP but not stored). This data cannot be used to identify individual users. No consent is required under GDPR for this type of processing.

2.4 Local Storage (Browser)

We store the following preferences in your browser's localStorage (not cookies):

  • shifa-lang — your selected language preference
  • shifa-cookies — your cookie banner dismissal choice
  • shifa-theme — your light/dark mode preference

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time via your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your contact form inquiries
  • To send you a confirmation email after form submission
  • To maintain website security and prevent abuse (rate limiting, bot detection)
  • To understand aggregate website usage (anonymous analytics)
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (Article 6 GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — when you voluntarily submit the contact form
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — for server logs (security), anonymous analytics (service improvement), and rate limiting (abuse prevention)
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — where required by law

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not set any cookies. Our website operates entirely without cookies. We do not use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any other cross-site tracking technology. Our analytics provider (Umami) is cookieless and does not collect personal data. No consent is required for our analytics under GDPR, the ePrivacy Directive, or the TTDSG (German Telecommunications-Telemedia Data Protection Act).

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your data may be processed by:

  • Hosting provider (cPanel shared hosting) — processes server logs on our behalf
  • Umami Cloud (umami.is) — receives anonymous, non-personal analytics data only
  • Email infrastructure (server-local Exim MTA) — delivers contact form emails; no third-party email service is used

We do not transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Our hosting and email infrastructure are located within the EU.

7. Data Security

We implement the following technical measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers to prevent injection attacks
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse (5 requests per IP per hour)
  • Bot detection via honeypot fields and timing analysis
  • Input validation and sanitisation on all form submissions
  • Header injection prevention on email sending

No system can guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable and proportionate steps to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

  • Contact form emails — retained in our inbox until your inquiry is resolved, then deleted within 90 days
  • Server logs — automatically deleted after 30 days by our hosting provider
  • Rate limiting data — IP hashes are automatically purged within 2 hours
  • Analytics data — aggregated and anonymous; retained indefinitely as it contains no personal data
  • localStorage data — remains on your device until you clear it; we cannot access or delete it

9. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15–22)

As a data subject in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@shifa.uz. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For Germany, this is the Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit (Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information).

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through our contact form, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (e.g., OpenStreetMap tiles in our map). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Material changes will be clearly indicated.

13. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or data subject requests regarding this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: contact@shifa.uz
  • Address: Hansastraße 116, 13088 Berlin, Germany
  • Website: shifa.uz
